About Us

Bridging Global Standards with Local Expertise

Technology for Cambodia was founded with a clear mission: to elevate Cambodia's manufacturing sector to world-class quality standards. We bring deep expertise from years of hands-on experience in semiconductor and electronics manufacturing.

Our founder brings extensive experience in quality management across semiconductor manufacturing, specializing in VDA 6.3 process auditing, Six Sigma methodology, and multi-functional quality leadership spanning SQE, IQC, SMT QA, NPI, ESD, Clean Room, and more.

We believe Cambodia's electronics manufacturing industry has enormous potential — and the right quality infrastructure is the key to unlocking it.
